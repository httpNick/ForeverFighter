<p>Larry Keaton, born in Anaheim California, discovered his love of music and performing arts watching his Father and Uncle entertain family and friends with their talents.
At an early age, Larry appeared in the plays Oklahoma, Fiddler on the Roof, and Go Ask Alice.
More recently, he performed in an improv group in Tacoma Washington. He studied screen writing at Pierce College and continues to write scripts today.
Playing both acoustic and bass guitar, Larry is a musician, an avid golfer, and enjoys a variety of outdoor activities such as running, fishing, and surfing.</p>
